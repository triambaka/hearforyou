ABOUT US

The main goal of this website is to create a safe place for people who need it and to also help in giving back to the community by letting people volunteer. This website is not essentialy a substitue for professional help. However, talking to people who know what you're going through will help immensily. One can either choose to sign up as a member or as a volunteer. upon choosing a path, they'll be asked to take a simple quiz that will determine the next step. One of our main selling points in anonymity. Each volunteer/member will have a unique user ID of their choosing. No personal information will be exchanged among the participants. We hope to help as many people as we can.
